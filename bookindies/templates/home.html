How We Design Your Book Cover

Here’s how we bring your book cover to life step by step.

Step 1

Dig Deeper Into Your Book Concept

We ask you to fill in a creative brief to understand your vision, catch the hook of your story, and reflect it in the cover design.

Step 2

Analyze Your Genre

We research typography, layouts, and color themes common to your genre to ensure the cover design will fit the market.

Step 3

First Sketch & Refinements
